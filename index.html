Our Story

How We Met

Lucy and Justin met in August of 2011 at a concert that was held for Mizzou students to welcome them for the new school year. They spotted each other from across the crowd and it was love at first sight. A couple months later, on October 16th, 2011, they began their relationship. Lucy and Justin graduated from the University of Missouri in May of 2015 together. On April 16th, 2016, Justin proposed to Lucy on Melbourne Beach, Florida. They spent the afternoon enjoying champagne on the beach to celebrate their engagement.
